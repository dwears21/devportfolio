Predicting US Bond Yields

This project enhances bond yield prediction by integrating additional features and using machine learning techniques. It builds on the research by Shu and Chou (2021), focusing on supervised methods like RNNs, LSTMs, and ARIMA, and unsupervised methods like Autoencoders and GANs. Key data sources include Yahoo Finance, FRED, BLS, and CBO. Improved bond yield forecasting aids investors, financial institutions, and policymakers in making informed decisions, managing risks, and formulating monetary policies.

This project analyzes Reddit posts from work-related communities to identify variations in sentiment and user engagement across different times and discussion types. It examines how discussion moods shift throughout the day, week, and month, identifies the most engaging topics or flairs within subreddits, and compares the relationship between post sentiment and user engagement. The goal is to uncover patterns in online interactions and provide insights into what influences discourse in digital communities.
